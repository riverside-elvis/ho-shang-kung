# Ch. 29 How to do nothing.

Who is going to want to take the empire,

> <div class="commentary">Who wants to become lord of the empire.</div>

and to manage it,

> <div class="commentary">Who wants to govern the empire by activity.</div>

I see that he does not get it.

> <div class="commentary">I see that he does not get the Tao of heaven to enlighten the mind of man. The Tao of heaven hates confusion and impurity. The mind of man hates the many desires.</div>

The empire is a spiritual tool. One cannot work with it.

> <div class="commentary">A tool is a thing. Thus men are the spiritual things within the empire. Spiritual things like peace and rest. They cannot be governed by activity.</div>

Who meddles with it spoils it.

> <div class="commentary">By governing it by activity one spoils its true nature.</div>

Who grasps it loses it.

> <div class="commentary">If one forcibly grasps it, one loses its essence and produces hypocrisy.</div>

Therefore of the beings, if one goes, the others follow.

> <div class="commentary">Where the prince goes, his subjects will certainly follow.</div>

Some are warm, others are cold.

> <div class="commentary">呵 hsü is warm, 吹 ch'ui is cold. If there are such as are warm, there are bound to be such as are cold.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 呵 v.1. 噓, same meaning.</div>

Some are strong, others are weak.

> <div class="commentary">If there are such as are strong and big, there must be others that are tender and weak.</div>

Some bear, others destroy.

> <div class="commentary">The bearers are peaceful, the destroyers are dangerous. If there are peaceful ones, there are bound to be dangerous ones. This explains why a prince cannot at the same time govern a country and practise asceticism.</div>

Therefore the saint flees from extremes, extravagances and excesses.

> <div class="commentary">Extremes are called yearning and lasciviousness, music and beauties. Extravagances are called garments and ornaments, food and drink. Excesses are called palaces and apartments, terraces and pavilions. By fleeing from these three things, by harmoniously walking within emptiness and by being without activity, the empire will change spontaneously.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of "garments and ornaments" v.1. "carriages and ornaments".</div>
