# Ch. 26 How to lay stress on Te.

Gravity is the root of lightness.

> <div class="commentary">If a prince is not grave, then he is not honoured. If asceticism is not taken seriously, then the spirits are lost. The blossoms and leaves of the herbs and trees are light, therefore they are perishable. The root is heavy, therefore it is lasting.</div>

>> <div class="note">The first sentence seems to allude to Lun-yü 1, 8: "If a gentleman is not grave, then he is not respected".</div>

Quietness is the master of motion.

> <div class="commentary">If a prince is not quiet, then he loses his dignity. If the ascetic is not quiet, then be endangers himself. The dragon is quiet, therefore he is able to transform. The tiger is restless, therefore he strives for heavenly faults.</div>

Therefore the saint walks about the whole day and does not separate from quietude and gravity.

> <div class="commentary">輜 (lit. baggage, baggage-waggon) = quietude. The saint walks the whole day in Tao, without departing from his quietude and gravity.</div>

Though he has palaces and gynaecea, he avoids them.

> <div class="commentary">榮觀 jung-kuan means palaces, 燕處 yen-ch'u (lit. swallows' nests) are the apartments of the princess and the concubines. He avoids them: he keeps far away from them and does not stay there.</div>

What remedy is there for the lord of the ten thousand carriages,

> <div class="commentary">奈何 nai-ho "what remedy is there" is an exclamation used by people suffering extremely from sickness. The lord of the ten thousand carriages is the king.</div>

if personally he makes light of the empire?

> <div class="commentary">The king is the most revered one, but if he is personally light-minded and unquiet in a sick time, then he will lapse into excessive luxuriousness and frivolous sensuality.</div>

If he makes light of it, then he will lose the officials.

> <div class="commentary">If the king is light-minded and excessive, then he will lose his officials. If the ascetic is light-minded and excessive, then he loses his semen.</div>

If he is restless, then he will lose the government.

> <div class="commentary">If the king falls a victim to the disease of restlessness, then he endangers his throne. If the ascetic falls a victim to the disease of restlessness, then he endangers his spirit.</div>
