# Ch. 60 How to remain on the throne.

Rule a large country as if you were cooking small fish.

> <div class="commentary">Hsien 鮮 are fishes. When cooking small fishes, the intestines are not taken out and the scales not scraped off. One does not dare to scratch them, for fear that they might go to pieces. When in governing a country one causes trouble, then the subjects will become confused. When in practising asceticism one causes trouble, then the spirit will become distrait.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 削 "scraped off" v.1. 去 "removed". Instead of 散 "will become distrait" v.1. 去 "will flee".</div>

When one manages the Empire with Tao, its demons do not appear.

> <div class="commentary">If one stays with Tao and Te on the throne, thus managing the empire, then the demons do not dare to make their spirituality visible in order to make men rebellious.</div>

>> <div class="note">V.1.: "then the demons do not dare to become offensive by means of their spirituality".</div>

Not only do its demons not appear; its ghosts also do not hurt men.

> <div class="commentary">Not only do its demons not show their spirituality, but wickedness does not enter goodness and is not able to hurt the nature of the people.</div>

>> <div class="note">An entirely different reading of the commentary runs thus: When a saint is on the throne, he does not hurt men. Therefore the demons do not molest them.</div>

Not only do its demons not hurt men, but neither does the saint hurt men.

> <div class="commentary">Not only are its demons and spirits not able to hurt anybody, but also by the saint on the throne nobody is hurt. Therefore the demons do not risk giving offence.</div>

Now when both do not hurt each other,

> <div class="commentary">The demons and the saint, both of them do not hurt each other.</div>

then Te will unite and return.

> <div class="commentary">As both do not hurt each other, man is able to govern the Yang, and the demon is able to govern the Yin. Man is able to fulfil his natural destiny, and the demon is able to save his spirituality. Therefore Te unites and returns.</div>
