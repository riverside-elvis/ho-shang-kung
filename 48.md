# Ch. 48 How to forget knowledge.

Who acts in learnedness, daily increases.

> <div class="commentary">Learnedness means to be learned in the doctrine of government, in pedagogics, ritual and music. If one daily augments the striving for culture, one largely increases day for day.</div>

Who acts in Tao, daily decreases.

> <div class="commentary">Tao the Tao of spontaneity is called. To decrease daily means that the desire for culture is daily reduced.</div>

By decreasing it decreases again.

> <div class="commentary">The decrease is the decrease of desire. The repeated decrease is that by which it slowly vanishes.</div>

Thereby one reaches non-action.

> <div class="commentary">One is to remain quiet like a little child. Thereby nothing is not acted.</div>

By doing nothing nothing remains undone.

> <div class="commentary">If desire is cut off, Te unites with Tao. Then everything spreads and is done.</div>

Who governs the Empire ought always to be without activity.

> <div class="commentary">取 (lit. to take) = to govern. Who governs the empire ought always to be without activity and must not give trouble.</div>

>> <div class="note">V.1.: "and not give trouble to the people".</div>

But who has activity is not sufficient to govern the empire.

> <div class="commentary">But who likes to be active, that one governs, teaches and harasses the people, so that they enjoy no peace. Therefore he is insufficient to govern the empire.</div>
