# Ch. 68 How to associate with heaven.

Those of old who were good warriors were not warlike.

> <div class="commentary">This means: Esteem Tao and Te and do not prefer martial strength.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 尙 "prefer" v.1. 好 "love".</div>

A good fighter does not become angry.

> <div class="commentary">Who fights well with Tao, suppresses the evil within his bosom. He smothers misfortune before it sprouts. He is angry about nothing.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 怨怒 "angry" v.1. 誅 |, with the same meaning.</div>

Who well vanquishes the foe does not contend.

> <div class="commentary">Who with Tao well vanquishes the foe, subjects the near ones by humanity and causes the far away ones by Te to draw near. He does not fight the enemy, but the enemy submits of himself.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 戰 "fights" v.1. 爭 "contends". Instead of 伏 "submits" v.1. 服 "surrenders".</div>

Who well uses others renders himself lowly,

> <div class="commentary">Who uses others well helps himself. Always he induces men to strive for humility.</div>

This is called the Te of non-contending.

> <div class="commentary">This means: What is said above causes that which follows. These are Tao and Te, as they do not contend with man.</div>

This is called using the strength of man.

> <div class="commentary">If one is able to follow this, Tao becomes associated with heaven.</div>

The highest goal of antiquity.

> <div class="commentary">This was the highest and most important principle of antiquity.</div>
