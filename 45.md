# Ch. 45 How to cause Te to overflow.

Great perfection seems broken.

> <div class="commentary">This means a prince entirely perfect in Tao and Te. Who seems broken annihilates the name and hides the praise. To seem broken is to be imperfect.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 若 "to seem" v.1. 如, meaning the same.</div>

Its use does not wear out.

> <div class="commentary">Who uses his mind in this manner, does not wear out but fulfils his time.</div>

Great fulness seems empty.

> <div class="commentary">This means a prince entirely filled out by Tao and Te. Who seems empty, though he be honoured, he does not dare being haughty. Though rich, he does not dare being prodigal.</div>

Its use is inexhaustible.

> <div class="commentary">Who uses his mind in this manner, is not to be exhausted.</div>

Great straightness seems bent.

> <div class="commentary">Great straightness means to cultivate Tao, to take measure for a model and to be correctly straight like unity. Who seems bent, does not contend with the vulgar, as if he were able to bend or to break.</div>

Great cleverness seems stupid.

> <div class="commentary">Great cleverness means to have many talents and accomplishments. Who seems stupid looks as if he did not dare to show his abilities.</div>

>> <div class="note">A variant of the last words, rejected by Li Ch'iao, runs thus: "does not show himself to be so".</div>

Great eloquence seems to stammer.

> <div class="commentary">A great orator is wise and does not falter. Who seems to stammer is unable to express himself.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of 凝 "falter" v.1. 疑 "doubt". A variant of the last sentence reads: "If wisdom entertains no doubts, the mouth will not speak".</div>

Motion overcomes cold.

> <div class="commentary">To overcome is the extreme. In spring and summer the Yang air moves quickly upwards, and all things become perfected. After the acme has been reached, it gets cold. If it is cold, then there is desolation, death and ruin. This means that man ought not to move quickly.</div>

Stillness overcomes heat.

> <div class="commentary">In autumn and winter all things remain quietly below the yellow springs. After the acme has been reached, it gets warm. Warmth is the source of life.</div>

Through clarity and stillness one becomes the regulator of the empire.

> <div class="commentary">If one is able to be clear and still, then one will become the head of the empire. If one keeps to what is right, one does not end one's time of life.</div>
