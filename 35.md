# Ch. 35 On the Te of humanity.

To him who holds fast to the great idea, the empire comes.

> <div class="commentary">執 = to keep. 象 the idea is Tao. If the saint keeps Great Tao, then all the people in the empire change their mind and return to him. If he practises asceticism, then heaven causes the spirits to descend and to have intercourse within his self.</div>

>> <div class="note">Instead of "all the people" v.1. "all the beings".</div>

It comes without being endangered. Peace and quietude are great.

> <div class="commentary">If all beings return without being endangered, then the empire is pacified and the families are peaceful, and the great peace is reached. If one practises asceticism without endangering the spirits, then the body is restful and extremely longlived.</div>

Where there is joy and beauty, the passing guest remains.

> <div class="commentary">餌 (lit. cake) = 美 beauty. The passing guest is unity. If a man is able to enjoy Tao, then unity remains. Unity leaves fulness and stays within emptiness. It appears suddenly like a passing guest.</div>

But if Tao comes out of the mouth, how insipid, O! is its tastelessness!

> <div class="commentary">When Tao leaves and enters the mouth, it is insipid and does not resemble the five tastes.</div>

>> <div class="note">Some editions of the Tao-te-ching read 言 "words" instead of 口 "mouth", but this reading is refuted by the wording of the commentary.</div>

If one looks for it, it is not sufficient to be seen.

> <div class="commentary">足 (to be sufficient) = 得 to be able to. Tao is formless and does not resemble the five colours that can be seen.</div>

If one listens for it, it is not sufficient to be heard.

> <div class="commentary">Tao does not resemble the five sounds that can be heard.</div>

If one uses it, it cannot become exhausted.

> <div class="commentary">If one uses Tao in governing a country, then the country is peaceful and the people are radiant. If one practises asceticism, then life is prolonged and there is no exhaustion of time.</div>
